Welcome to the Home Page of PerCuss!

The above photo courtesy of FreeDigitalPhotos.net

PerCuss! incorporates a Marching Band drum set.

PerCuss! is a alternative rock band composed of students who took CS493/CS593 Computer-assisted Music Generation in the Spring of 2012. This album is composed of the final projects of the students. All instrumentals (with perhaps the exception of Mr. Powe's compostiion) were composed and generated using the Songlib C Music Library.

The views, opinions, conclusions, and lyrics expressed in this page are those of the student artists and not necessarily those of The University of Alabama or its officers and trustees. The content of this page has not been reviewed or approved by The University of Alabama, and the artists are solely responsible for its content.
